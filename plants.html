Bambusa

Bambusa is a large genus of clumping bamboos.Most species of Bambusa are rather large, with numerous branches emerging from the nodes, and one or two much larger than the rest.

Orchid

Orchids are plants that belong to the family Orchidaceae a diverse and widespread group of flowering plants with blooms that are often colourful and fragrant.

Gaillardia

Gaillardia (common name blanket flower) is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, native to North and South America.
